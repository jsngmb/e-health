🧠 Mental Health Fundamentals

1. What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It directly impacts how we think, feel, react, handle stress, make life choices, and maintain interpersonal relationships throughout our life stages.

2. Why is it Important?

It is essential because it dictates physical resilience, boosts focus and study capability, balances emotional intelligence, and allows us to meaningfully connect and share with our local family or peers without burnout.

3. How to Deal with it?

Dealing with mental health friction requires clear, actionable coping mechanisms:

  • Open Expression: Share your thoughts immediately with a trusted teacher, guidance counselor, or friend.
  • Paced Breathing: Slow down nervous responses using structural 4-second box breathing routines.
  • Healthy Routines: Protect dynamic hours for deep sleep and cut off high digital screen times before resting.
